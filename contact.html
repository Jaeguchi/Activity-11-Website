FROM AUCKLAND CBD

1. Take Southern Motorway

2. Take off ramp exit toward East Tamaki Road

3. Turn left at East Tamaki Road (traffic lights)

4. Take the 1st left onto Otara Road (traffic lights)

5. For Gate 12: Continue through next intersection (traffic lights)

6. Turn right into MIT Gate 12.

7. For Gate 13: turn right at the next intersection into Bairds Road

8. Take the first left into Alexander Crescent

9. Turn left into Gate 13.

2. Take off-ramp exit toward East Tamaki Road

3. Turn left at East Tamaki Road (traffic lights)

4. Take 1st left onto O tara Road (traffic lights)

5. For Gate 12: Continue through next intersection (traffic lights)

6. Turn right into MIT Gate 12.

7. For Gate 13: turn right at the next intersection into Bairds Road

8. Take the first left into Alexander Crescent

FROM INTERNATIONAL AIRPORT

1. Take Tom Pearce Drive (Highway 20B), go through 1 roundabout

2. At next roundabout take 1st left onto Puhinui Road

3. Turn left at Wyllie Road

4. Turn right at Station Road

5. Turn left at Shirley Road, go through 1 roundabout.

6. Turn right at Great South Road (traffic lights)

7. Turn left at East Tamaki Road (traffic lights)

8. Continue until you reach Otara Road, turn left (MIT signage on corner at major intersection)

9. For Gate 12: Continue through next intersection (traffic lights)

10. Turn right into MIT Gate 12.

11. For Gate 13: turn right at the next intersection into Bairds Road

12. Take the first left into Alexander Crescent

13. Turn left into Gate 13.
